What The Hack Is TVH?

Tshwane University Hackthon is an annual Hackathon hosted by Tshwane University of Technology aimed at challenging Information and Communication Technology (ICT) students from various
Tshwane Metro learning institutions to demonstrate their skills in software development, cloud computing,
and agile practices while competing to solve industry and community problems using technology.
